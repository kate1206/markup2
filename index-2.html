More families choose us

to care for the ones they love.

As the largest and most respected provider of senior living services in New England, Benchmark’s life’s work is helping seniors live happier, healthier and more fulfilling lives.

We offer a complete continuum of individualized care: Assisted Living, Memory Support, Rehabilitation Services and Skilled Nursing. What makes Benchmark really different, however, is that we provide a higher staff-to-resident ratio than typical senior living communities. This extra level of care ensures that every resident achieves the highest quality of life possible.

Your family becomes our family.

At Benchmark, we’re committed to finding new ways to keep residents and their families connected. We are always developing innovative new programs, services and technology to keep you in touch and informed of your loved one’s health and well-being.

Our focus on your family’s peace of mind is another reason why Benchmark is different from any other senior community—anywhere in the country.

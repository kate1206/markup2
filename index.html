Introducing the Benchmark Lifetime Rate Promise.

We will never raise your monthly rent—for life!

Your parents promised to cherish and care for each other for a lifetime. At Benchmark, we have this same committment. We are here if someone you love needs a little—or a lot—of extra help to take advantage of the possibilities still ahead.

With 50 exceptional senior living communities across New England, so many families place their confidence in Benchmark to provide a fulfilling and carefree life for the ones they love.

Now this worry-free way of life extends to financial well-being: The Benchmark Lifetime Rate Promise. When someone moves to or reserves a residence in a Benchmark community by June 1st, we promise that the monthly rent will never increase. For a lifetime.

So now seniors can plan ahead and live with greater peace of mind.
